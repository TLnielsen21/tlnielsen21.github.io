It’s nice to open my first github blog….!

My past professional work, and passions have involved :

  1. Entrepreneurial project management in real estate, and medical device sales – 17 years

  2. K-16 education – I hold a K-12 Principal license, & K-12 Special Ed Director license, specialties in Literacy & STEM for Gifted Learners, English Lang. Acquisition – ELL students, & School Psychology – Gifted, Autism, .. + similar

First Day of Ruby Programming Class – 7 – 15 – 2013 –

I’m spending as many spare minutes as I can carve out, researching my “aggregate reason for wanting to learn to code.”

  1. Though I recently sold my “Co-founding Board Member” financial interest in a Medical Sales company, I hope to continue working in the Medical field as part of a consultative team helping a hospital or physician practice successfully transition to use of EMRs, EHRs – Electronic Medical Records / Electronic Health Records.

  2. The physicians / hospital group that I hope to open a working relationship doing this – feel overwhelmed about all that is involved.

While learning Jason Noble’s Building the Ruby Tool-Belt course, I have a burning desire to learn the Ruby Coding Language, within the medical EHRs context.

Now that I understand the world of EMRs better (than I knew them a week ago) ….

I am better positioned to dive into Chris Pine’s key concepts outlined in Learn to Program, 2nd Ed.

I hope that you will want to check-in with my posts along my way to Building My Ruby Tool-Belt ….!
